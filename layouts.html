LAYOUTS

Cute Theme comes with a sophisticated layout system to create any kind of sidebar or widget layout based on Warp Framework. You can easily manage the sidebar’s positions and modules in the theme administration. Modules can have different styles and be placed in any position offered by this theme. Each position has its own layout. You can align modules side-by-side, stack them or choose your own grid layout. Show or hide modules on mobile devices, while the grid adapts perfectly to your layout.
